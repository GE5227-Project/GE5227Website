HO Shi Yun

Educator | GIS Practitioner | Data Scientist

Introduction

Ho Shi Yun is a professional geography educator for several years, and she is a postgraduate in Applied Geographical Information System (GIS) at the National University of Singapore (NUS). Prior to pursuing her postgraduate studies, she has done undergraduate studies at National Institute of Education with a major in Geography and a minor in English Linguistics. Her interests include critical GIS, biopolitics, and migrant-related issues. Her thesis will be focusing on the spaces of negotiations carved by Chinese migrant workers in Singapore.

Aside from her proclivity towards hotpot and all things spicy, she is a big fan of musical theatre (both Broadway and West End) and the crosstalk comedian troupe Deyunshe. She was once involved in musical productions in college and now wishes to be able to snatch a ticket at the elusive crosstalk show put up by Deyunshe. Her limited experience with web development started off from her childhood obsession with modifying blog layouts to be pink and sparkly in her formation years.

LYU Wenling

GIS Practitioner | Photography Enthusiast

Introduction

Lyu Wenling graduated from Beijing Forestry University with a major in Urban Planning. Having cumulated some experience as an urban planner shortly after her graduation, Wenling was actively involved in a multitude of projects related to Smart City and Web GIS. She is now currently pursuing postgraduate studies in Applied Geographical Information System (GIS) at National University of Singapore.

Born in Jiangsu, China, Wenling has a penchant for seeking for the hippest food joints and a weakness for all things Cantonese. She’s slowing acquiring a tongue for spicy food after arriving in Singapore and hopes to be able to seek out more delicacies in this sunny island.

While she believes that she’s still young in her journey in developing as a coder, she believes that her experience in this project has unveiled her inclinations towards UI/UX web design and she looks forward to learning more.

HOU Yujun

GIS Practitioner | Artist | Foodie

Introduction

Hou Yujun obtained her undergraduate degree with a major in Environmental Studies and a minor in GIS from NUS in 2020, and is currently pursuing a master's degree in Applied GIS at NUS. Her interest lies in urban sustainability and planning and is keen on exploring different technologies to study urban patterns and sustainability-related issues.

She is a great fan of hiking, playing the harmonica, and growing plants whenever there’s pockets of free time in her schedule. She, too, likes to consider herself to have an inclination for cooking – or for some staunch cooking aficionados – to be capable of creating somewhat edible sustenance for herself in her free time. Despite being born and raised in a hometown of light (yet tasty) cuisine, Yujun’s trip to Chongqing, China, has proved to be a pivotal point in her life. She has since then developed a taste for fiery food and now demands for more Sambal chilli paste along with her Nasi Lemak at the Nasi Padang Stall.

Before coming to the world of Internet GIS, Yujun has explored different programming languages including Python, R and Java.

Jeremias LIEW

Coding Enthusiast | Classical Musician | Gamer

Introduction

Jeremias Liew is currently a postgraduate student in Applied Geographical Systems (GIS) at the National University of Singapore (NUS). Prior to his masters, Jeremias completed his undergraduate degree with a major in Environmental Studies and minor in GIS. His academic interests include urban climates, climate change, and remote sensing.

Adding on to his impressive multi-disciplinary background is his active involvement in the music scene. Trained with a background in classical music, Jeremias has been part of several symphony orchestras in Singapore and has performed overseas as part of music festivals. Aside from his disposition towards classial music, Jeremias enjoys listening to a wide myriad of music genres and languages, from Shostakovich's 5th Symphony to the likes of Lee Ronghao and even Metallica. Jeremias has some experience in Python scripting and Java software engineering, and enjoys doing coding challenges on online judge websites during his free time.

He is the residential helpdesk on all things related to GitHub for other group members in this group.
